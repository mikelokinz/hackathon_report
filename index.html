Strategic Recruitment Insights

01

DSA Testing Obsolescence

Generative AI has degraded standard whiteboard DSA assessments. Hackathons are moving heavily towards complex systems architecture, functional programming (e.g. Juspay), and AI agents integration under strict resource limits.

02

Rise of Agentic AI

Recruiting organizations (like EY Techathon 6.0) seek engineering candidates capable of designing autonomous, goal-driven agentic architectures rather than simple reactive API automations.

03

Risk Mitigation Co-ops

The standard 6-month co-op window (July 1 – Dec 31) acts as a structural probation period. It provides organizations sufficient time to validate candidate codebase contributions before giving Pre-Placement Offers.

04

Diversity as an Advantage

Programs like Myntra's HackerRamp (WeForShe) sepurve as high-fidelity diversity funnels, bypassing campus placement limits to secure top female technical talent at the design/architectural level.
